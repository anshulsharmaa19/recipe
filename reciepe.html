Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the milk powder, all-purpose flour, and ghee. Mix well.
  2. Add warm milk gradually and knead to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Bring to a boil and simmer until the sugar dissolves completely. Add cardamom powder and set aside.
  4. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls.
  5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the dough balls until they turn golden brown.
  6. Remove the fried balls and immediately immerse them in the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Serve the gulab jamun warm or at room temperature.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Grind the cashew nuts into a fine powder using a food processor or blender.
  2. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Bring to a boil and simmer until the sugar dissolves completely.
  3. Add the cashew powder to the sugar syrup and mix well to form a smooth dough.
  4. Add ghee and cardamom powder to the dough and mix thoroughly.
  5. Grease a flat surface or a tray with ghee. Roll out the dough into a thin layer using a rolling pin.
  6. Cut the rolled dough into diamond or square shapes using a knife.
